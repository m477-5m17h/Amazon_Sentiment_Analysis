About This Project

The Amazon Review Sentiment Analysis project aims to enhance decision-making for marketing strategies and product development in the e-commerce industry by providing a detailed analysis of customer sentiments expressed in Amazon product reviews. By examining the sentiment distribution, word clouds, and common adjectives used in reviews, this project helps identify key areas of customer satisfaction and areas needing improvement.
